PART ONE – OVERVIEW

      Entering the job market is intimidating to many job seekers and newly graduated college students. It does not have to be this way. Our goal is to give job seekers knowledge they can use to make the job hunt less painful. When someone is searching for an employment opportunity they will likely take into account the industry, company, duties, salary, location, and many other features. The purpose of this project is to gather, explore, and analyze job listing data to provide job seekers with insight on the skills that are most sought after for full-time data scientist, data engineer, data analyst, and business intelligence positions across the United States.

      Starting from scratch, we decided to get our data by scraping popular job postings websites , on which we will conduct Natural Language Processing and Text Mining techniques to dig deep into the skill set information, then we will relate it to geographic information by comparing and contrasting skill requirements in different states and east and west coast.
